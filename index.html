About Susan

Susan Gravdahl Parsons has studied bellydance for over 10 years with professional bellydance artist Tava Naiyin and Irina Akulenko. She has taught fitness classes as a certified SharQui Bellydance Workout instructor and was certified by Oreet Jehassi Schwartz. Susan helped her body heal through bellydance after a severe windsurfing accident and back surgery. Susan's teaching goal is to promote health, wellness and total body acceptance of each member of her classes. She was a member of Tava's Manipura Dance Troupe. If you would like more information about classes, please email Susan at susan@liliyanabellydance.com
